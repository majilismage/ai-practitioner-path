What You'll Achieve

By the end of this programme, you'll move from using AI as a fancy chatbot to building AI-powered systems that multiply your effectiveness. You'll understand not just what to build, but when and how to build it — with the confidence that comes from hands-on experience.

Weekly Coaching Sessions

Each week includes a 30-minute coaching session with Jake. Here's what to prepare:

Week 1: Kickoff, context, first exercise together
Week 2: Review vibe-coded app, discuss mental models
Week 3: Cowork live demo, troubleshoot setup
Week 4: Review Skill definition, discuss agent architecture
Week 5: Final review, next steps
